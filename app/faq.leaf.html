htmlbin FAQ

How do I use images?

Either get them hosted elsewhere - or, if the images are less than 32K, embed them with a data URI using base64 encoding.

How do I edit a page that is already hosted?

Make a new one! You may do this as many times as you like. If you want to have a persistent URL, use web forwarding with your domain.

How long will my page be hosted?

Until it is classed as inactive - no visits for a long time.

How do I delete a page?

You don't need to - inactive pages are deleted.

Will my page be ad-free?

Yes!

Will my page be indexed by search engines?

Only if you link to it from other sites. Bots cannot crawl htmlbin pages.
